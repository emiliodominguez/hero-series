/ how it works · 4 steps

brief to shipped, in four moves.

i.week 0

Quick brief

A short call (or doc). We agree on the shape of the thing.

~ 30 min
ii.week 1—2

Direction

Two distinct directions. We pick one and sharpen it together.

~ 8 days
iii.week 3—5

Build

Daily commits, weekly reviews, async loop. No surprise reveals.

~ 14 days
iv.week 6

Hand-off

Files, docs, a 15-minute walkthrough. You own everything.

~ 1 day
