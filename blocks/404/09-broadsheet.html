Page not found: a national mystery deepens.

Servers stand empty, hyperlinks dangle, and one curious URL has gone missing without leaving a forwarding address.

By a Staff Reporter · Filed at 23:47

Late on Sunday evening, an anonymous request for a page known only by its URL was received at our front-of-house terminal. The page, sources confirm, is not where it was last filed — nor where any reasonable archivist might think to look. A search of the basement, the file room, and three editors' desks turned up nothing of substance.

"It was right here," said the night editor, gesturing vaguely at a wall. "I'm certain of it. I'd staked my reputation on it being on this server, somewhere between /about and /contact. Now it's just — gone."

Authorities are urging the public not to panic. Most pages, they say, are still accounted for. The rest, they admit, may simply have moved on.
